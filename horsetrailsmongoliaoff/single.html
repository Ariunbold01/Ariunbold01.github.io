Steppe trails ride

Amartuvshin T. | 2017-01-11 06:02:51

Our base-camp is located south of the Bogd Khan National Park, just a few miles from Ulaanbaatar. Here you can ride through the mountains and on the Steppe. You will have the chance to ride through the Mongolian countryside, meet nomadic herders, visit their ger.

Bogd Khaan Uul, on the southern edge of the Siberian taiga zone, was proclaimed a national park in 1778. Other attempts to conserve the area occurred as early as the 12th century. Now designated the Bogd Khan Strictly Protected Area with 42,000 hectares, UNESCO has proposed another wildlife park of up to 65,000 hectares. Bogd Khan is immediately south of the Tuul River, south of Ulaanbaatar and west of Nalaikh. It surrounds Tsetsee Gun Peak and contains Zaisan Memorial, Nukht Valley and the Manzshir Monastery.
